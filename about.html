Charles Donoghue

Co-Author

Charles has helped thousands of people throughout the world take control of their lives, strengthen their belief system, and build a good self-image.

From a background of poverty and violence, and having to leave school at fifteen to help his solo mother, he has become a Performance Psychology Coach, author, and a much sought-after international speaker. He has featured on TV and radio in several countries, had his own slot on TVNZ's Good Morning Show, and spoken at the International Business College and Foshan University in China.

He is the author of several books on personal development and motivation. Charles has shared the same platform as Dr Edward de Bono, Dr Alex McKenzie, and others, and has accumulated numerous accolades from high-profile people, authors, and successful people from all walks of life.

Glenn Edley

Co-Author

Glenn Edley is the Founder and Email Marketing Coach at Spike, and the author of the original MondayMotivator — a weekly inspirational email to kickstart thinking he sends to thousands of avid readers every Monday without fail.

He has been featured in many business magazines and newspapers, spoken on radio, and presented workshops for Digital Boost and the Mailchimp Masterclass series.

Glenn has always been passionate about helping businesses convert their customers into cash by sending better email. He believes having consistent and clear communication with your clients and customers is the best way to stay top of mind, sell stuff, and keep your customers.

He also believes you are an experiment of one — and you should always be testing your beliefs and habits to see if they are working for you.
